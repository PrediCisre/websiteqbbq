Our Story

Q BBQ Public House is Cambridge, Ontario’s first smokehouse, serving up those southern favourites with a side of fresh corn bread. From our Sweet Caroline Burger to our AAA New York Steak, everything is smoked, cooked and prepared to perfection. Enjoy that southern comfort food in your own backyard when you come to Q BBQ Public House. We use apple wood and hickory for all our smoking to give it that deep southern flavour. We smoke all our meats low and slow to create that beautiful smoke ring that is true southern BBQ. This may create the meats to sometimes look pink and undercooked. We would like to reassure you that all meats are cooked to proper temperature.
